About TKAgency

Founded by Terrence Tungamirai Kufandimbwa, a qualified accountant with extensive experience in business operations, TKAgency is dedicated to helping businesses achieve their goals through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships.

With a background in accounting and a passion for business development, I established this agency to provide comprehensive services that address the complex challenges faced by modern businesses in technological, financial, and retail sectors.

Our mission is to act as trusted agents, representatives, facilitators, and intermediaries for the promotion, distribution, and delivery of products and services while providing administration and management services that ensure efficient, innovative, and customer-focused solutions.
